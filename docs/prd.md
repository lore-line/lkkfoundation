Phase: Planning → MVP Build

1\. Overview

Purpose



Build a modern, trustworthy foundation website that:



Educates the public



Strengthens brand integrity



Showcases philanthropic efforts



Converts visitors into donors, volunteers, and partners



Core Narrative



Inspired by Lim Ket Kai’s legacy of protection, generosity, and livelihood-building, the foundation continues to nourish the land and grow the sprouts — investing in youth, extending compassionate care, and building accessible futures.

7\. Scroll Animation Spec (Integrated)

Feature: Nourish \& Grow Scroll Animation



Purpose:

Visually express growth, continuity, and impact.



Placement:

Home page between Legacy and 3 Pillars.



Behavior:



Seed → stem growth → leaves appear → full plant.



Progress tied to scroll depth.



Section height: ~200–300vh.



Sticky center layout.



Symbolism:



Soil → Legacy



Stem → Foundation continuity



Leaves → 3 Pillars



Accessibility:



Respects prefers-reduced-motion.



Static fallback state.



Performance:



Lightweight SVG implementation.



No heavy animation libraries required for MVP.
